    The School Church

    Was established in 1978

    The School Church is a unique concept that combines the values and traditions of the Christian faith with educational institutions. It serves as a moral and spiritual anchor, providing students and staff with a place to reflect on the meaning of life and seek guidance during difficult times. The School Church also integrates Christian values into the academic curriculum, promoting empathy, compassion, forgiveness, and ethical behavior. By integrating the spiritual with the academic, the School Church enriches the learning experience and encourages empathy and understanding of different beliefs and traditions.



    The School Library

    Was built in 1980

    The School Library is an essential component of any learning institution, serving as a source of knowledge, research, and creativity for students and faculty. The library offers a wide range of resources, including books, journals, magazines, and online databases, providing students with the tools they need to explore new ideas, complete assignments, and pursue their interests.



    The School Auditorium

    Was built in 1982

    The School Auditorium is a crucial component of every school, providing students with a platform to showcase their talents and abilities. It is a multipurpose space that can be transformed into a stage for plays, concerts, recitals, and other performances. With its elaborate lighting, sound system, and seating arrangements, the Auditorium creates an immersive cultural experience that stimulates creativity and expression.

    Achievements

    Inter-School debate on Environment Pollution

    The achievement of winning the Interschool Environment Pollution Debate is a remarkable feat that demonstrates the student's knowledge, communication skills, and passion for the environment. The debate is a platform where students can engage in discussion and debate, argue their points, and learn about different perspectives on environmental issues. Winning the debate requires thorough preparation, research, and a deep understanding of the topic at hand. It also requires the student to think quickly on their feet and express their ideas and opinions in a clear and persuasive manner. This kind of critical thinking and communication skills are valuable in both academic and professional settings and demonstrate the student's commitment to the environment.

    Inter-school Football Competition

    Winning the interschool football competition is a great achievement for a student. It requires not only physical fitness but also teamwork, communication, and leadership skills. The student who won this competition must have demonstrated these skills to excel in the competition. This achievement will not only boost the student's self-esteem but also instill the value of determination, perseverance, and hard work in other students of the school. In addition, the student's win will boost the school's reputation and encourage students to pursue their interests in sports and other activities.

    Interschool Science Competition

    Winning the interschool science project competition is a significant accomplishment for a student, as it reflects their creative problem-solving skills, research abilities, and passion for science. Participating in such a competition requires extensive research, preparation, and presentation skills, as the students must design and execute a scientific project and communicate their findings to a diverse audience.In terms of educational outcomes, the achievement of winning the science project competition will enhance the student's knowledge and understanding of scientific principles, develop their research and communication skills, and encourage their interest in pursuing scientific fields in the future. The student will gain valuable experience in dealing with public speaking and presenting their ideas confidently, which will be useful in future endeavors.
